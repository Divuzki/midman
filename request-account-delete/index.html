We're sorry to see you go. Here's how to request account deletion.

How to Delete Your Account

To ensure the security of your account and verify your identity, we require you to submit a deletion request via email. Please send an email to our support team with the details listed below.

Required Information

Please include the following in your email:

  • Account Full Name: The name registered on your profile.
  • Account Email Address: The email associated with your account.
  • Account Phone Number: The phone number linked to your account.
  • Reason for Deletion: Briefly explain why you wish to delete your account.
Email Support to Delete

Clicking this button will open your default email client.

Note: Upon receiving your request, our team may contact you to verify your identity before processing the deletion. Some data may be retained for legal and compliance purposes as outlined in our Privacy Policy.
